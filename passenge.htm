Click The Ear for Real Audio Commentary
  • In my last BMW, with these woofers, we kept the plastic covers that came
    with the car. They rattled because the 13" is very powerful, so we decided
    it was time for steel kick panels with carpet to eliminate all rattles. The idea
    was to keep the stock look. Another reason for the steel was that I take off
    the cover during my judging presentation, and wood would get a little rough
    after a while.
  • Photo Descriptions.
    Henry shaped metal mesh material into the same shape as the original covers first.
    Bondo was applies to only leave the grille area open for sound, then sanded smooth.
    A BMW grille from the rear deck was then fitted into place and then carpeted with
    factory carpet. Notice the finished factory look.
