• The 370 pounds of Dynamat used in this car play  a very important role in the
    final sound quality of the total system. When the car is on the road, the noise
    level is much lower then it was when I got the car. The finished Dynamat, look
    also makes the car look more finished during judging.
  • Photo Descriptions.

    1 layer of Dynamat Flex light was melted and formed into shape.
    1 layer of undercoating was applied for a clean finished look.
