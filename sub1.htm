The new B & W Nautilus system uses a newly designed 15" hollow magnet
woofer. To keep the sound as close as the original designer wanted, I kept
the 15 inch. A tough fit but after some listening, well worth it.  Anyway when
is the last time you saw a regular sedan with 2 15" subs in that location?
Size matters!!!!!!!!!
